About The Station Festival

Step Into the Groove at Our Station!

Celebrate music, art, and community in the vibrant heart of Spain at the legendary Parc del Fòrum. Join a global gathering of music lovers and world-class artists for an unforgettable journey of rhythm, inspiration, and connection.
